Welcome to Mariata Homes Charitable!

Mariata Homes is a charity organization that offers subsidized accommodation to members of the public. Their clients are usually people from lower socio-economic backgrounds, but occasionally, they receive some unexpected middle-class income brackets clients who at that instance may need assistance to find a roof over their heads; as such, clients do not stay for long (usually a few weeks).
